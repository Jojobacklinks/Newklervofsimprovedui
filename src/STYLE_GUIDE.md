# Field Service Management Dashboard - Style Guide

## Color Palette

### Primary Brand Colors
- **Orange**: `#f0a041`
- **Blue**: `#28bdf2`
- **Purple**: `#c555e1`
- **Green**: `#b9df10`
- **Red**: `#f16a6a`

### UI Colors
- **Text/Headings**: `#051046` (All text and headings throughout the application)
- **Primary Button Background**: `#9473ff`
- **Primary Button Hover**: `#7f5fd9`
- **Border Color**: `#e2e8f0` (Cards and popups)
- **Form Field Border**: `#e8e8e8`

## Global Styling Rules

### Cards & Popups
- **Border Radius**: `20px`
- **Border Color**: `#e2e8f0`
- **Shadow**: `rgba(226, 232, 240, 0.5) 0px 2px 16px 2px` (soft shadows)

### Form Fields
- **Border Width**: `1px`
- **Border Color**: `#e8e8e8`
- **Border Radius**: `15px`

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Poppins (Applied globally to all headings, text, and every text element)
- **Text Color**: `#051046`

### Buttons
- **Primary Background**: `#9473ff`
- **Primary Hover**: `#7f5fd9`
- **Border Radius**: `32px` (All buttons throughout the application)
